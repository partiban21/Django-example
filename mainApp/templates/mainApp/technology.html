LG 4K OLED TV

LG has been the market leader for OLED TV's, as they are the only company able to produce OLED screens on a large scale. Hence why other companies are buying their screens from LG to make their own line of OLED TV's.

LG has produced some stunning televisions using OLED, which produce deep levels of black unparalleled by QLED that Samsung uses in comparison. However, OLED is suseptible to screen burn in when left on an static image for too long.
