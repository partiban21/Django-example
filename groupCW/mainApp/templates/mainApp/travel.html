Los Angeles Is The Place For Heat

It is currently 32 degrees in L.A, where this temperature is seen and felt constantly, establishing the fact that if you love warm places, this may be the place to go.

Aside from the heat, there are many places to see included but not limited to; Hollywood hills, stars (if you happen to see them), stunning beaches such as Venice beach and much more. L.A is a popular tourist attraction for its heat, oppurtinities and Hollywood. However, tickets are not cheap.

Cheap Deals To South Africa

Emirates Voted Best Airline

The airline has been voted the best airline to travel from by survey conducted on their customers.

The poll included questions such as, "have you flown with another airline before" and "would you fly with us again", with more than 70% agreeing that Emirates delivered great food for even passengers flying in economy, as well as great customer service on par with Etihad and Qatar airways. This cements Emirates as one of the elite airlines to fly with.
